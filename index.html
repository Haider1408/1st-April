My Love ❤️

From the moment you came into my life, something in me changed in a way I didn’t fully understand at the time. You weren’t just someone I met—you became the feeling I learned to recognise as home. Before you, love was something I had heard about, something I didn’t quite know how to hold. But with you, it started to make sense. In the quiet moments, in the laughter, in the little things we shared without even realising, you taught me how to care, how to show up, and how to love with intention. We never had that “proper” moment where I asked you to be my girlfriend. It all came naturally, as if our hearts already knew what our words hadn’t caught up to yet. But even so, I’ve always felt that you deserve to be chosen, clearly and wholeheartedly, without hesitation. So let me say it now, in the way you deserve to hear it: I choose you. Not just in the way we’ve already been, but in the way I continue to be drawn to you every day. I want you as my girlfriend, not by accident, but by choice—because of who you are, and because of what you bring into my life. You are beautiful, kind, supportive, and full of a warmth that stays with me even when you’re not around. So my love, will you make me the happiest man on Earth and be my girlfriend?
