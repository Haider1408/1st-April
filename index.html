Dear Sheza 💖

Your heart was my first. I didn’t even know what I was doing in a relationship at the start, but I learned—with you, and for you. I tried to do all the little things, the cute efforts… but I never properly asked you to be my girlfriend, because we just felt the same way.

And honestly, you deserve so much more than that, my love.

Today marks a whole year of you being my girlfriend, and I just want to say thank you. Thank you for being so beautiful, kind, supportive, loving, caring, charismatic, funny… and so much more than I could ever put into words.

So this time, I want to ask you the right way.

Sheza Shah, over this past year, I’ve felt nothing but love and happiness with you in our highs, and devotion and trust in our lowest moments. I want to keep feeling that with you—for as long as life allows.

Forever is a long journey…

But right here, right now—until forever comes—

Will you make me the happiest man alive and be my girlfriend? 💖
